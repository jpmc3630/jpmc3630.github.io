About Me

I am an aspiring full stack developer that has recently found my passion in improving the digital lives of people by writing code that makes a difference. I have been coding at an amateur level since I was a kid, but last year returned to study to take things to the next level, brushing up on the latest web technologies and learning the current industry standards.

I enjoy the human experience and user journey aspects of front end development, as well as the technical problem solving challenges of back end development. Ultimately, I love bringing these two aspects together to deliver smooth full stack applications that exceed client expectations.

Aside from web development, I have a background in media and information and am also interested in taking on media related freelancing work such as digital content production.
